Why Calendly

Everything you need to own your calendar

One shareable link

Your personal booking page lives at a clean URL. Share it anywhere — email, bio, Slack — and let guests choose their slot.

Smart availability

Set your weekly hours once. Calendly automatically blocks off times you're already booked and respects your buffers.

Instant notifications

Guests get a confirmation email the moment they book. You get notified too — with all the details, ready to go.

Multiple event types

Create as many meeting types as you need — quick syncs, deep dives, consultations — each with its own duration and link.
